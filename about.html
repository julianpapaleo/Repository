ABOUT US


MATEÏ (Matei LLC) was founded in September of 2022 by Julian Papaleo and Sylvestre Corti. The
company believes in boosting the world one healthy cup of mate at a time by becoming the first Retail
Yerba Mate Store. The drink, traditional from Argentina and nearby countries in South America, is an
excellent caffeine option as it provides energy while also delivering nutrients, vitamins and antioxidants.
It is our aim to deliver an experience that honors Yerba Mate while powering the next generation by
increasing productivity and socialization. We want you to EXPERIENCE MATEÏ, DRINK MATEÏ, BE MATEÏ.
